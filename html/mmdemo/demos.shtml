MMBase demos

Welcome to MMbase the quick unzip & go version of the MMBase Content management system.

This is a full working MMBase setup that comes with several simple applications auto-deployed. Its goal is allow people to use MMBase within minutes of downloading the system and see what its all about. Its also handy to test applications without using your production machines/setups.

The MMbase Team.
